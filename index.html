About the Maker

Three decades of hands-on craftsmanship.

Matt Built is a one-man workshop run by Matt — a carpenter and joiner with 35+ years of experience in bespoke kitchens, fitted furniture and custom cabinetry. Every project is designed around your space and built by hand, with no shortcuts.

From the first sketch through to the final hinge, you work with Matt directly — so what's drawn is what gets made, and what gets made is built to last.
